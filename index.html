Famous Quotes from Popular Footballers that motivates our life

Football is a the most popular game in the World.It is played accross more than 200 countries and is watched by billions and billions of fans around the globe.FIFA is the international governing body of association football, beach soccer, and futsal which is responsible for organizing the various tournaments notably the famous FIFA mens World Cup.

Football has produced many popular sportsmen who have made an entire generation fall for them with their amazing footballing skills.They have made themselves global brands and their incredible life story have inspired billions of people worldwide not only in the field of sports but in every profession.

Here in this article we have listed some famous quotes by those legends which will surely inpire and motivate your life.

Success is no accident. It is hard work, perseverance, learning, studying, sacrifice and most of all, love of what you are doing or learning to do.” ~ Pele

“You have to fight to reach your dream. You have to sacrifice and work hard for it.” – Lionel Messi

“Your love makes me strong, your hate makes me unstoppable!”~ Cristiano Ronaldo

“I once cried because I had no shoes to play soccer, but one day, I met a man who had no feet.” ~ Zinedine Zidane

“There is no pressure when you are making a dream come true.” ~ Neymar Jr.

"You come up against challenges in life,and it's how you deal with them that defines you"~ Harry Kane
